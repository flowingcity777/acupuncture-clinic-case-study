Designing a Calm and Trustworthy Acupuncture Clinic Website

Overview

This project focuses on designing a website for an acupuncture clinic offering traditional Chinese medicine services. The goal was to create a clear, calm, and trustworthy digital experience that helps patients understand the clinic’s services and request appointments with ease.

Problem

Many healthcare websites feel overwhelming, unclear, or overly technical. Patients often feel uncertain before their first visit:

  • What does acupuncture involve?
  • What should they expect?
  • How do they book an appointment?

Goals

  • Build trust through calm communication
  • Make services easy to understand
  • Reduce uncertainty
  • Provide simple booking flow
  • Keep system manageable for clinic owner

Constraints

  • No backend system
  • Limited technical resources
  • Need simple solution
  • Healthcare communication must be responsible

Process

Focused on clarity over complexity:

  • Defined user needs
  • Structured content clearly
  • Designed calm layout
  • Simplified booking into request form

Solution

  • Calm homepage
  • Clear service structure
  • “What to Expect” section
  • Appointment request form
  • Accessible contact info

Key Decisions

  • Acupuncture as primary focus
  • Request-based booking
  • Minimal, calm design
  • Reduce patient uncertainty

Outcome

The result is a clear, trustworthy website that supports patients while remaining simple for real-world use.

Reflection

This project reinforced the importance of designing for real-world constraints and prioritizing clarity over complexity.
