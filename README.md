# Acupuncture Clinic Website — UX Case Study

A UX case study focused on designing a calm, trustworthy website for an acupuncture clinic offering traditional Chinese medicine services.

---

## 🌿 Overview

This project explores how thoughtful design can improve clarity, trust, and usability in a healthcare setting.

The goal was to create a digital experience that helps patients:
- understand the clinic’s services
- feel more comfortable before their first visit
- request appointments easily

---

## 🎯 Problem

Many healthcare websites feel overwhelming, unclear, or overly technical.

For this clinic, new patients often had questions such as:
- What does acupuncture involve?
- What should I expect during a visit?
- How do I book an appointment?

The existing experience did not clearly guide users or reduce uncertainty.

---

## ✨ Solution

The final design includes:

- A calm, welcoming homepage
- Clear service descriptions with acupuncture as the primary focus
- A “What to Expect” section to guide first-time patients
- A simple appointment request form (instead of complex booking)
- Clear and accessible contact information

---

## 🧠 UX Focus

This project emphasizes:

### Trust & Clarity
Using calm, neutral language to support patient confidence without making exaggerated claims.

### Reducing Uncertainty
Helping users understand the process before their first visit through structured content.

### Simplicity for Real Use
Designing a request-based booking flow that is easy for both patients and the clinic owner.

---

## 🧩 Key Design Decisions

- **Acupuncture as the primary focus**  
  Reflects what most patients seek, even though the clinic also offers herbal support.

- **Request-based appointment flow**  
  Keeps the system simple and flexible without requiring backend complexity.

- **Calm, minimal interface**  
  Reduces cognitive load and supports a healthcare-appropriate tone.

- **“What to Expect” section**  
  Addresses common concerns and builds trust before booking.
